MLB Hall of Fame Predictor

Overview

We have chosen to build a system that, given the lifetime statistics of a Major League Baseball position player, predicts whether or not that position player will be inducted into the Hall of Fame. We construct two Gaussian models: one that is representative of Hall of Famers and another that is representative of players not in the Hall of Fame.
